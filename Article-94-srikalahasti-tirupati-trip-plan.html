  • Distance: 38 km (Srikalahasti → Tirupati)
  • Travel time: 45-60 minutes by road
  • Transport: Bus (₹50-₹80), Taxi (₹800-₹1000), Auto (₹500-₹600)
  • Combined trip: Easily doable same day (Srikalahasti AM + Tirupati PM)

  • Best plan: 2-day trip — Day 1 Srikalahasti, Day 2 Tirumala darshan

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Without online booking Rahu-Ketu puja attempt: Peak days (Saturday, Tuesday) = 3-4 hour wait without booking. 200+ people in walk-in queue. Online book = direct entry at slot time. ₹0 extra cost for booking — just advance planning।
    • 👉 इसे भी पढ़ें: TTD ePass guide
  • ❌ Same day both temples + return plan: Technically possible but exhausting. Srikalahasti (3-4 hours) + travel (1 hour) + Tirumala darshan (4-6 hours) + travel = 12-15 hour marathon. Better: 2 days. Day 1 Srikalahasti + Tirupati rest. Day 2 Tirumala fresh।
  • ❌ Srikalahasti local transport ignore: Temple से bus stand/railway station = 2 km. Walking hot afternoon = tiring. Share auto ₹10-₹20 — available freely. Or auto rickshaw ₹50। Small expense, big comfort difference।
  • ❌ Tirupati direction opposite plan: Chennai/Bangalore highway → Srikalahasti → Tirupati = sequential (efficient). Tirupati first → Srikalahasti → back Tirupati = backtracking 76 km extra. Plan route smart।
  • ❌ Puja documents incomplete: Rahu-Ketu puja — birth details needed (date, time, place). Pandit asks nakshatra/gotra. Prepare before — ask family elders if don't know. Without details puja still happens, but less personalized।
