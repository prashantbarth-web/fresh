Clothing Checklist

Item Purpose Quantity Notes
Traditional outfit (darshan) Temple dress code requirement 1 set (clean, pressed) Men: dhoti/pants+shirt. Women: saree/salwar
Comfortable walking clothes Travel day, sightseeing 1-2 sets Breathable cotton — Tirupati hot
Comfortable sandals/chappals Easy remove at temple + walking 1 pair (worn) Broken-in — no new shoes
Socks (thin cotton) Temple floor hot afternoon (summer) 1-2 pairs Optional but useful
Light jacket/shawl Tirumala hill cool mornings (15-20°C) 1 Essential Nov-Feb / early morning queue
Rain poncho (monsoon) June-Sept — sudden rains common 1 (disposable OK) Umbrella also works
Undergarments (extra) Change after tonsure/trek/sweating 2-3 extra Compact pack
Small towel/dupatta Wipe sweat, cover head (sun), multipurpose 1-2 Gamcha style best — light, quick dry
💡 Insider Tip: Sandals > Shoes for Tirupati trip। Temple entry = footwear remove। Shoes = carry burden all day, space in locker needed। Sandals = slip off, slip on, easy। Walking comfort still good if quality sandals (broken-in Bata/Relaxo type)। Running shoes = unnecessary weight।

Food & Snacks Checklist (Queue Survival Kit)

Item Why Quantity Note
Water bottle (reusable) Hydration — free RO refill points 1 per person (500ml-1L) Refillable — RO taps on hill
Glucose biscuits Quick energy in queue 3-4 packets Parle-G type — compact
Mixed dry fruits Sustained energy, healthy 200g pack Almonds, cashews, raisins mix
Toffees/Candy Quick sugar boost, kids 10-15 pieces Eclairs, Pulse type
Namkeen (small pack) Savory snack — salt replacement 2-3 small packs Haldiram mini packs ideal
Juice pack (Frooti/Real) Quick energy + hydration 2-3 packs (200ml each) Tetra pack — no spillage
⚠️ जरूरी बात: Queue (3-6 hours) में no food stall access। Whatever carry करेंगे = that's your survival kit। Under-pack करना सबसे common mistake। Family of 4, 5-hour queue = minimum 8 biscuit packs + 4 water bottles + dry fruits + juice packs। Over-prepare > under-prepare।

💡 Insider Tip: Small sling bag (cross-body) = best for Tirumala trip। Backpack too bulky for queue (people behind push into it). Sling bag front-facing = secure, compact, accessible. Keep: phone, snacks, water, medicines, ID — that's all you need in queue. Large bag → locker deposit (free)।

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Aadhaar photocopy न ले जाना: Original Aadhaar lose हो गया trip में? Photocopy backup। TTD कभी-कभी photocopy भी accept करता है verification के लिए (rare case — original best)। 2 copies ज़रूर carry।
    • 👉 इसे भी पढ़ें: vegetarian restaurants guide
  • ❌ New shoes/sandals पहनकर जाना: New footwear = blisters guaranteed (2-3 km walking)। Trip से 1-2 weeks पहले wear करके break-in करें। Old comfortable sandals = best choice।
  • ❌ Power bank carry न करना: Full day activity (12-16 hours active) — phone battery dies by afternoon। Queue में navigation, communication, emergency = phone ज़रूरी। 10000 mAh power bank = lifesaver।
  • ❌ Only UPI/card depend करना: Tirumala hill top small shops, autos — cash preferred। Network issues common on hill (crowd congestion)। ₹500-₹1000 cash (₹10, ₹20, ₹50 notes) carry। UPI backup — but cash primary।
  • ❌ Heavy luggage carry करना: Large suitcase = burden। Tirumala trip = 1-2 days max। Small backpack + sling bag sufficient। Heavy bag = locker dependent, carrying tiring, stealing risk। Pack minimal — wash and repeat।
