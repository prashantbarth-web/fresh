Food Hygiene Tips

  • ✅ Established restaurants: Google reviews 3.5+ stars prefer करें
  • ✅ Sealed water: Bottled water या TTD RO taps ही use करें
  • ✅ Hot food: Fresh cooked, garam food खाएँ — cold stored avoid
  • ✅ TTD canteens: Safest option — government controlled hygiene
  • ❌ Street cut fruits: Pre-cut displayed fruits avoid — bacteria risk
  • ❌ Roadside stalls: First 1-2 days avoid — stomach adjust होने दें
  • ❌ Very spicy food immediately: South Indian spice level different — gradually try

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ TTD Annadanam ignore करना: बहुत pilgrims को पता ही नहीं कि free unlimited meals available हैं। Quality excellent — hygienic South Indian meals। Use करें — money save + experience gain।
  • ❌ South Indian food बिना try किए reject करना: "Idli sambar नहीं खाते" — कम से कम try तो करें! First time अजीब लग सकता है, but 2nd-3rd time से enjoy करेंगे। Curd rice safest start।
  • ❌ Very spicy food immediately खाना: Andhra food India's spiciest है — North Indians का stomach handle नहीं कर पाता initially। Mild options से start: curd rice, plain dosa, pongal।
  • ❌ Empty stomach queue join करना: "Darshan ke baad khayenge" — 3-6 hours empty stomach queue = weakness, dizziness। Light breakfast ज़रूर खाएँ before queue।
  • ❌ Random street food try करना Day 1: Stomach travel fatigue + new water + new food = trouble। First 1-2 days established restaurants only। Street food बाद में (when adjusted)।
