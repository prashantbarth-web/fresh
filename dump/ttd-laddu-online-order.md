<h2>What is the TTD Proforma Laddu?</h2>
The laddu you get at the Tirumala counter is the "Prasadam Laddu" — handed out after darshan, on the hill, within the per-person limit.

The "Proforma Laddu" is the system through which a pilgrim can order TTD's authentic laddu without travelling to Tirumala — that is, from home — and receive it by delivery.

It is the very same Srivari Laddu: same recipe, same kitchen, same GI-tagged product. Only the packaging is different, made courier-safe for transit.

💡 <strong>Insider Tip:</strong> Do not let the word "Proforma" confuse you — it is not a lower grade. It is the same laddu made in TTD's Potu (kitchen), just packed to survive a courier journey.

<h2>Step-by-step — how to order online</h2>
<h3>Step 1: Go to the official portal</h3>
Open tirumala.org → "Online Services" → "Proforma Laddu Booking."

⚠️ <strong>Important:</strong> Only tirumala.org is the official and authentic source. The third-party sites that show up when you Google "Tirupati Laddu Order" are not affiliated with TTD, and ordering from them will not get you authentic prasadam.
<h3>Step 2: Login / registration</h3>
If you already have a TTD account, log in. To create a new one:
<ul>
 	<li>Enter your mobile number</li>
 	<li>Verify the OTP</li>
 	<li>Fill in basic details — name, email, address</li>
</ul>
<h3>Step 3: Fill in the order details</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Select the quantity — how many laddus you want</li>
 	<li>Enter the delivery address accurately — pincode, city, state</li>
 	<li>In some cases there is an option to note an occasion — optional</li>
</ul>
💡 <strong>Insider Tip:</strong> Always add a landmark to the address — despite the pincode, courier services deliver more reliably with a clear landmark.
<h3>Step 4: Payment</h3>
UPI, net banking and debit/credit cards are all accepted, at about ₹200 per laddu (delivery already included). On success you get an order confirmation by email, tracking details by SMS — note down the Order ID.
<h3>Step 5: Track delivery</h3>
A tracking link from TTD's courier partner arrives by email. Delivery takes about 3–7 working days, varying by location.

<h2>Shelf life and storage</h2>
The Proforma Laddu's shelf life is 7–10 days from the delivery date. So if delivery takes 5 days and the shelf life is 7 days, consume it within 7 days of receiving it.
<ul>
 	<li>Room temperature: 7–10 days — keep in a cool, dry place.</li>
 	<li>Refrigerator: up to about 15 days, though the texture changes slightly.</li>
 	<li>Freezer: not recommended.</li>
 	<li>Wrapping in plastic helps protect it from moisture.</li>
</ul>
⚠️ <strong>Important:</strong> In peak summer (April–June), North Indian room temperatures of 40°C+ can spoil the laddu quickly — refrigerate it. For carrying laddus when you do travel, see the <a href="/tirupati-packing-list/">Tirupati packing list</a>.
<h2>How many can you order at once?</h2>
TTD updates the per-order limit from time to time, based on demand — it tightens before festivals and is more generous off-season. For the current limit, the portal itself shows the maximum quantity at checkout.

💡 <strong>Insider Tip:</strong> If you need a larger number, plan ahead and order within the portal's stated limit rather than trying to work around it — TTD sets the limit to keep the prasadam available to everyone fairly.

<h2>What if delivery fails or the laddu arrives damaged?</h2>
Keep your Order ID and the payment confirmation safe — both help with any complaint. If a damaged or wrong item arrives, report it quickly (generally within 24 hours) with a photo, through the support channel on the official TTD portal.

⚠️ <strong>Important:</strong> Use only the contact and grievance channels listed on tirumala.org — avoid phone numbers or emails forwarded on social media, which are often fake.
<h2>❌ Common mistakes</h2>
<ul>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Ordering from a third-party site:</strong> not every site that ranks first on Google is TTD. Use only tirumala.org.</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Not calculating delivery time:</strong> with a 7–10 day shelf life, work backwards from when you will actually consume it.</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Ordering two days before a festival:</strong> for Diwali or Navratri gifting, order three weeks ahead — processing and delivery both slow down.</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Filling the wrong address:</strong> a wrong pincode or house number causes failed delivery and a complicated refund. Double-check it.</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Forgetting to refrigerate in summer:</strong> in the heat, do not leave it at room temperature for more than 3–4 days or it turns sticky.</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Not saving the Order ID:</strong> you need it for both tracking and any complaint — screenshot the email confirmation.</li>
