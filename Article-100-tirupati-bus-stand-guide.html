"Bus stand कहाँ है? Tirumala bus कौन सी? Timings? Last bus?" Tirupati में 2 main bus stands हैं — city bus stand (APSRTC) और Tirumala bus stand (hilltop)। Confusion normal — guide follow करें। मैंने हर trip में APSRTC bus use की — cheapest, reliable, और frequent। Complete bus navigation share कर रहा हूँ। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — Tirupati Bus Stand:
  • Main Bus Stand: APSRTC Tirupati Bus Station — city center
  • Tirumala buses: Every 10-15 min from APSRTC stand, ₹75-₹150
  • First bus Tirumala: ~4:00 AM
  • Last bus Tirumala: ~10:00 PM (varies seasonally)
  • Journey time: 45-60 min (ghat road, 22 km)
  • From station: 500m walk from Railway Station to APSRTC bus stand

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Auto-wale से "bus band hai" believe करना: Classic Tirupati scam — auto drivers say "aaj bus nahi chal rahi" to get ₹600-₹1000 fare। IGNORE. Walk to bus stand yourself — buses run 4 AM to 10 PM daily without fail। APSRTC government service — never "band"।
  • ❌ Peak hours (8-11 AM) Tirumala bus lena: Maximum crowd = 30-60 min just bus queue। Then 45 min ride। Then darshan queue 4-6 hours। Total 6-8 hours without darshan! Better: 4-5 AM bus (10 min wait) = reach early = darshan queue shorter too।
  • ❌ Return bus timing ignore करना: "Darshan ke baad dekhenge" — darshan 8 PM finish, last bus 9 PM. Cutting it close! If any delay = stranded. Plan: finish darshan by 7 PM = comfortable return। Or plan overnight stay on hill (free choultry available)।
  • ❌ Wrong bus stand जाना: Tirupati has multiple stands. Tirumala buses = APSRTC main bus station (500m from railway station). NOT town bus stand (local routes only). Ask "Tirumala bus stand" — locals will point correctly. Google Maps: "APSRTC Bus Station Tirupati"।
  • ❌ AC bus expect करना har time: AC buses limited frequency — Non-AC more frequent. AC ₹100-₹150, Non-AC ₹75. Summer = AC preferred (worth wait). Winter/monsoon = Non-AC fine (windows open, breeze). Don't wait 30 min for AC if Non-AC available immediately।
