  • Official source: TTD official website (tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in) से ही order करें
  • Process: Login → Laddu Booking section → Quantity select → Address fill → Payment → Delivery
  • Price: ₹50/laddu (approximate) + delivery/courier charges extra
  • Delivery: India Post/Courier — 3-7 working days (location dependent)
  • Quantity limit: Limited per order (check current policy — usually 5-10 laddus max)
  • Availability: Subject to TTD production capacity — sometimes temporarily unavailable

TTD Laddu — क्या Special है

Feature Details
Official Name Srivari Laddu (श्रीवारि लड्डू)
Weight ~175 grams per laddu
Ingredients Bengal gram dal, sugar, cashews, cardamom, ghee (pure cow ghee)
GI Tag Yes — Geographical Indication protected
Daily Production ~3-4 lakh laddus (Potu — TTD kitchen)
Shelf Life ~15 days (without preservatives)
Unique Factor Made in Tirumala Potu (temple kitchen) — blessed prasadam
💡 Insider Tip: TTD Laddu preservative-free है — इसीलिए shelf life limited (15 days) है। Online order करने पर fresh batch ship होता है — delivery time calculate करके order करें ताकि fresh खा सकें। Received होने के बाद refrigerate करें — room temperature पर 5-7 दिन, fridge में 12-15 दिन तक fresh रहता है।

Online Laddu Booking कैसे करें — Step-by-Step

Step 1: TTD Official Website पर जाएँ

  • Website: tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in
  • ⚠️ सिर्फ official website से order करें — third-party websites/apps avoid करें

Step 2: Login / Register

  • Existing account: Mobile number + Password से login
  • New user: Register करें (name, mobile, email, address)
  • OTP verification complete करें

Step 3: Laddu Booking Section ढूंढें

  • "Prasadam" या "Laddu Booking" section navigate करें
  • Menu/Dashboard में available — location varies based on website updates
  • "Online Prasadam Booking" या similar option click करें

Step 4: Quantity Select करें

Detail Information
Minimum order 1-2 laddus (check current policy)
Maximum order 5-10 laddus per order (varies)
Price per laddu ~₹50 (TTD subsidized rate)
Multiple orders May be restricted per account per month

Step 5: Delivery Address Fill करें

  • Complete address with PIN code
  • Mobile number (delivery person contact करेगा)
  • Landmark helpful — especially smaller cities
  • Delivery available across India (most PIN codes)

Step 6: Payment करें

  • Laddu cost + Packing charges + Delivery/Courier charges
  • Payment modes: UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking
  • Payment successful → Confirmation email/SMS

Step 7: Tracking और Delivery

  • Order confirmation के बाद tracking details मिलते हैं
  • India Post या authorized courier से delivery
  • Delivery time: 3-7 working days (distance dependent)
  • Delivered at doorstep — sign required
⚠️ जरूरी बात: Laddu online booking subject to availability है — TTD production capacity limited है और demand बहुत high। Festival seasons में service temporarily suspended हो सकती है। Website regularly check करते रहें — slots open होने पर immediately book करें।

Charges Breakdown

Component Approximate Cost Notes
Laddu cost ~₹50 per laddu TTD subsidized price
Packing charges ₹50-₹100 per order Special insulated packing
Delivery charges ₹100-₹300 (distance based) India Post/Courier — varies by location

💡 Insider Tip: Group order करें — 4-5 families मिलकर एक order दें (maximum quantity), फिर आपस में distribute करें। Per-family delivery charges बच जाएँगे। Plus fresh batch एक साथ आएगा।

Fake Sellers से बचें — Warning

Real (TTD Official) Fake (Avoid)
Website: tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in Random websites claiming "TTD Laddu delivery"
TTD official app Third-party apps on Play Store
Government prescribed pricing ₹200-₹500 per laddu (overpriced)
Authentic GI tagged product Duplicate laddus (similar looking but different)
Official tracking via India Post No tracking, cash-on-delivery demands
  • 🚫 Amazon/Flipkart पर "TTD Laddu" listings — ये official TTD से नहीं हैं
  • 🚫 WhatsApp/Facebook sellers — fraud common है
  • 🚫 Railway station vendors (outside Tirupati) — mostly duplicate
  • 🚫 कोई भी "bulk discount" offer — TTD bulk retail नहीं करती individuals को
💡 Insider Tip: Authentic TTD Laddu identify करना: specific taste (cardamom + ghee dominant), weight (~175g), packaging (TTD logo + details printed)। Duplicate laddus usually ज़्यादा sweet, oily, और lighter होते हैं। सिर्फ TTD official source से order करें — guaranteed authentic।

Laddu Received होने के बाद — Storage Tips

Storage Method Duration Tips
Room temperature (winter) 5-7 days Cool dry place, original packing में
Room temperature (summer) 2-3 days AC room preferred, consume quickly
Refrigerator 12-15 days Airtight container; room temp पर लाकर खाएँ (taste better)
Freezer 1+ month Possible but texture changes; thaw before eating

Alternatives — अगर Online Available नहीं है

  • TTD Trust offices: Major cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad, Bangalore) में TTD liaison offices — कभी-कभी laddu available
  • Tirupati जाने वाले किसी से मँगवाएँ: Personal network — request extra laddus buy करके लाने को
  • TTD Kalyana Mandapam (branches): Some TTD Kalyana Mandapams in cities may distribute on special occasions
  • Wait for availability: Online service periodically resume होती है — TTD website check करते रहें

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Third-party websites से order करना: सिर्फ TTD official website (tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in) ही authentic source है। Amazon, Flipkart, random websites — सब duplicate या unauthorized हैं। Fraud और quality issues common।
  • ❌ Summer में order करना (May-June): Transit में 40°C+ temperature — laddu spoil हो सकता है बिना refrigeration। Winter (Nov-Feb) या monsoon best time है online ordering के लिए।
  • ❌ Delivery address incomplete देना: PIN code गलत, landmark missing — courier return हो सकता है। Perishable item return = waste। Complete accurate address दें।
  • ❌ Receive करके store न करना properly: Laddu preservative-free है — open रखने पर 2-3 दिन में spoil। Receive होते ही refrigerate करें (अगर तुरंत खाने वाले नहीं हैं)।
  • ❌ बहुत quantity order करना एक बार में: Shelf life 15 days है — 20 laddus order करके 15 दिन में खत्म नहीं कर पाएँगे तो waste। Need-based quantity ही order करें।
