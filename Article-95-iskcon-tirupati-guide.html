"ISKCON कहाँ है? Timings? Darshan कैसा?" ISKCON Tirupati = Sri Sri Radha Govinda Temple — beautiful, peaceful, well-maintained। Tirumala darshan के crowd से different — यहाँ calm atmosphere, kirtan, और international standard temple experience मिलता है। मैंने 2 बार visit किया — both times refreshing experience। City center से accessible, 1-2 hours sufficient। Complete visiting guide share कर रहा हूँ। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — ISKCON Tirupati:
  • Location: Hare Krishna Land, Tirupati (city — not Tirumala hill)
  • Distance: 4-5 km from Railway Station
  • Timings: 4:30 AM - 1:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 8:30 PM
  • Entry: Free — no ticket needed
  • Deity: Sri Sri Radha Govinda (Krishna-Radha)
  • Time needed: 1-2 hours (darshan + temple tour)

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Afternoon 1-4 PM visit attempt: Temple closed — deity rest period. Many tourists reach 2-3 PM and find gates closed. Frustrating — wasted auto fare. Check timings: morning till 1 PM या evening 4 PM onwards।
  • ❌ ISKCON confuse करना Tirumala temple se: Completely different — ISKCON city में है (Krishna temple), Tirumala hill पर है (Venkateswara/Vishnu)। Both Vaishnava but separate. Visit both — different experience, different location।
  • ❌ Very quick 15-minute visit: Temple beautiful — worth slowing down. Sit in hall 15-20 min, feel the kirtan energy, explore garden, visit book store. Rushing in/out = missing the point. ISKCON = peace, slow experience।
  • ❌ Shorts/inappropriate clothing: ISKCON also has dress code — modest clothing required. Same as Tirumala rules mostly. Men: full pants + shirt. Women: salwar/saree. No sleeveless, no shorts।
  • ❌ Govinda's restaurant miss करना: Temple visit + restaurant skip = 50% experience missed। Food excellent, affordable, sattvic। Especially North Indians — roti/sabzi missing करते हैं Tirupati trip में — Govinda's solves that。
