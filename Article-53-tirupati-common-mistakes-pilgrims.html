  • #1: Advance booking न करना (train, hotel, darshan ticket) — slots/rooms last-minute नहीं मिलते
  • #2: Wrong dress code — shorts, sleeveless, mini skirts = entry deny
  • #3: ePass download न करना — booking confirmation ≠ ePass
  • #4: Travel day ही darshan plan करना — exhaustion = bad experience
  • #5: पानी और food insufficient carry करना — queue में dehydration/hunger

15 Common Mistakes — Detailed List

Mistake #1: Advance Booking न करना

What Goes Wrong Impact Solution
Train ticket last-minute book करना Seats full, Tatkal fail, trip cancel 120 days advance book (IRCTC)
Hotel advance book न करना Walk-in पर rooms full या 2x price Online book (MakeMyTrip/OYO) 2-3 weeks पहले
Darshan ticket advance न लेना ₹300/₹500 slots भरे, 6+ hours free queue TTD website पर release होते ही book
TTD room advance book न करना Tirumala पर room नहीं मिलता 1-2 months advance TTD website से book
💡 Insider Tip: "Planning starts 2-3 months before trip" — यही golden rule है। Tirupati one of India's most visited destinations है — demand always exceeds supply। Last-minute planning = stress + compromise + overspending।

Mistake #2: Wrong Dress Code

Not Allowed Allowed (Men) Allowed (Women)
Shorts / Bermudas Dhoti + Shirt Saree
Sleeveless tops Full pants + Shirt (tucked) Salwar Kameez / Churidar
Mini skirts / Short dresses Kurta Pajama Long skirt + Blouse (covered)
Torn/ripped jeans Lungi (white) Anarkali / Traditional dress
Transparent/revealing clothes Formal pants + Full shirt Any modest traditional attire
⚠️ जरूरी बात: Wrong dress code = entry deny। No exceptions। Security strictly check करती है। अगर wrong clothes में आ गए, तो temple area में dress rental shops हैं — ₹50-₹100 में dhoti/saree rent कर सकते हैं। लेकिन better — पहले से proper clothes पहनकर जाएँ।

Mistake #3: ePass Download न करना

बहुत लोग booking confirmation SMS/email को ePass समझ लेते हैं। Wrong!
  • Booking confirmation = सिर्फ reference number
  • ePass = PDF with QR code — actual entry proof
  • Gate पर सिर्फ ePass (QR code) accept होता है
  • Website/App से अलग से download करना ज़रूरी है
Fix: TTD website → My Bookings → Download ePass → Print + Phone save

Mistake #4: Travel Day ही Darshan Plan करना

30+ hours train journey → directly Tirumala → queue join = disaster recipe
  • Body exhausted — 30 hours sitting/lying
  • Mind tired — sleep deprivation
  • Queue में 3-6 hours और — total breakdown
  • Darshan experience = suffering instead of spiritual
Fix: Travel day arrive → rest → next day fresh darshan

Mistake #5: Water और Food Insufficient Carry करना

What to Carry Why Minimum Quantity
Water bottles Queue 3-6 hours, dehydration risk 2-3 liters per person
Light snacks Energy maintain, hunger avoid Biscuits, dry fruits, banana
ORS/Glucose Electrolyte loss in heat 2-3 packets
Wet wipes/Towel Sweat, hygiene 1 pack + small towel

Mistake #6: Festival Season में Unprepared जाना

October-January = peak season। Crowd 3-5x normal days। बिना preparation जाना:
  • Train tickets unavailable/expensive
  • Hotels full/overpriced
  • Queue 8-12 hours (instead of 3-4)
  • Tirumala roads jammed
Fix: Festival season — 2-3 months advance plan। या off-season जाएँ (Feb-Mar, Jun-Aug)।

Mistake #7: Auto Driver की Advice Follow करना

Tirupati auto drivers commission-based hotels, restaurants, और shops recommend करते हैं:
  • Hotels: Overpriced rooms (driver gets ₹100-₹300 commission)
  • Shops: Fake/low-quality "silk" sarees और religious items
  • Restaurants: Average food, inflated prices
Fix: अपनी research करें। Hotel advance book करें। Google Maps use करें navigation के लिए।

Mistake #8: ID Proof भूल जाना

TTD darshan verification पर original ID proof mandatory है:
  • ePass पर जो name है — same ID proof carry करना ज़रूरी
  • Photocopy accept नहीं होती — original चाहिए
  • सभी family members का individual ID ज़रूरी
  • ID number booking में match होना चाहिए
Fix: एक folder में सभी members के original IDs + photocopies रखें। Checklist बनाएँ before leaving hotel।

Mistake #9: Mobile Phone Temple Area में Use करना

  • Temple sanctum sanctorum area में photography strictly banned
  • Phone use पर security confiscate कर सकती है
  • Selfie attempt = removal from queue
  • Camera, tripod, professional equipment = not allowed inside
Fix: Temple outer area में photos लें। Inside — spiritual moment enjoy करें, phone pocket में रखें।

Mistake #10: Reporting Time Late पहुँचना

ePass पर reporting time = deadline, suggestion नहीं:
  • Late पहुँचने पर entry deny हो सकती है
  • Slot miss = no refund (most cases)
  • Reporting time से minimum 30-60 minutes पहले पहुँचें
Fix: ePass reporting time से 1 hour पहले reporting point पर पहुँचें। Traffic delays calculate करें।

Mistake #11: Tirumala पर Private Hotel Search करना

Tirumala hill top पर कोई private hotel/lodge exist नहीं करता — सब TTD controlled है। Google पर "Tirumala hotels" search करने पर जो results आते हैं — वो Tirupati city के हैं या scam listings। Fix: Tirumala stay = TTD guest house / choultry only। Private hotel चाहिए = Tirupati city में book करें।

Mistake #12: Cash Insufficient Carry करना

  • Tirumala पर ATMs limited हैं — long queues
  • कई shops/services cash-only हैं
  • UPI network issues common (hill area connectivity)
  • Emergency situations — cash ही reliable
Fix: ₹3,000-₹5,000 cash carry करें per person। Tirupati city ATM से पहले withdraw करें।

Mistake #13: Return Ticket Advance Book न करना

जाने की ticket book की, वापसी की नहीं — classic mistake:
  • Tirupati से return trains equally busy हैं
  • Station पर general ticket queue 1-2 hours
  • Last-minute return flights expensive (₹8,000+)
Fix: दोनों तरफ की tickets (onward + return) एक साथ book करें।

Mistake #14: Darshan के बाद Tirumala Explore न करना

90% pilgrims darshan → laddu → bus → Tirupati — बस! But Tirumala पर:
  • Silathoranam (65 million year old natural arch)
  • Akashganga Teertham (sacred waterfall)
  • Sri Venkateswara Museum
  • Papavinasanam Dam, Gogarbham viewpoint
Fix: Darshan के बाद 2-3 hours extra रुकें — free attractions explore करें। Trip complete feel होगी।

Mistake #15: Children/Elderly की Special Needs Ignore करना

Need Mistake Solution
Children — restless in queue Entertainment plan नहीं रखना Books, games, snacks, headphones carry
Elderly — standing difficulty Long queue force करना ₹300/₹500 ticket (shorter queue) या wheelchair service
Children — food preferences Assuming restaurant food available Pack familiar food from home
Elderly — medical needs Medicines forget करना Full medical kit + extra medicines carry
Infants — diaper/feed needs Queue में facilities assume करना All baby supplies carry — diapers, formula, bottles
