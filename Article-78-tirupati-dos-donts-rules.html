"कौन से rules हैं? क्या पहनें? Mobile ले जा सकते हैं? Photography allowed है?" Tirumala temple strict rules follow करता है — centuries-old traditions + modern security protocols दोनों। कुछ rules ignore करने पर entry denied हो सकती है, कुछ पर fine लगता है। 5+ visits में मैंने देखा — बहुत pilgrims unknowingly rules break करते हैं, फिर embarrassment होती है। This guide covers every important do and don't — ताकि आपकी yatra smooth और respectful हो। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — Key Rules:
  • Dress code: Traditional/modest — no shorts, no sleeveless, no ripped jeans
  • Mobile phones: Allowed to carry — but SWITCHED OFF inside sanctum
  • Photography: BANNED inside temple — outside campus OK
  • Footwear: Remove before temple entry — free stands available
  • Non-veg/Alcohol: Completely banned on Tirumala hill
  • Queue discipline: Strict — pushing, cutting = removed from queue

Complete DOS — ये ज़रूर करें

# DO This Why Important
1 Traditional/modest dress पहनें Entry requirement — inappropriate clothing = entry denied
2 ID proof carry करें (Aadhaar/Passport) Darshan ticket verification — mandatory for all adults
3 Early morning पहुँचें (3-4 AM) Shortest queues — 2-3 hour wait vs 6-8 hours afternoon
4 Light snacks + water carry करें Queue 3-6 hours — empty stomach = weakness, dizziness
5 Bags locker में रखें before queue Large bags not allowed in queue — free lockers available
6 Mobile silent/off करें temple inside Respectful + security rules — ringing = embarrassment
7 Queue discipline maintain करें Breaking queue = removed by TTD staff — serious
8 Patience रखें — divine timing Rush/frustration = bad experience। Calm mind = better darshan
9 Comfortable footwear पहनें Walking 3-5 km total — sandals recommended (easy remove)
10 Prasadam counter पर laddu collect करें Free 2 laddus included with darshan — miss mat करें

Complete DON'Ts — ये बिल्कुल ना करें

# DON'T Do This Consequence
1 Photography/Video inside temple Phone confiscated — returned after darshan
2 Shorts/sleeveless/ripped jeans पहनना Entry denied — need to change clothes
3 Non-veg food carry करना hill पर Confiscated at checkpoint
4 Alcohol/tobacco carry/consume Confiscated + possible legal action
5 Queue में push/fight करना Removed from queue — restart required
6 Fake ID/ticket use करना Criminal offence — police case possible
7 Temple premises में shoes पहनना Not allowed — must remove at designated area
8 Sanctum में ज्यादा time spend करने की कोशिश Staff push forward — thousands waiting behind
9 Electronic gadgets (camera, tablet) carry Not allowed in queue — locker use mandatory
10 Unregistered vehicles hill top park करना Only registered/permitted vehicles allowed — fine
💡 Insider Tip: Dress code enforcement strict है — especially women के लिए। Salwar kameez, saree, full-length kurti + leggings = perfect। Men: dhoti, full pants + shirt (tucked in looks respectful)। Jeans allowed — but torn/ripped jeans NOT allowed। Safest bet: carry one traditional outfit specifically for darshan day।

⚠️ जरूरी बात: अगर inappropriate clothes में पहुँच गए — don't panic। Tirumala पर TTD stalls dhoti/saree rent/buy कर सकते हैं (₹50-₹200)। Temple entry point पर available — last resort option। लेकिन better prepared आएँ — time और embarrassment बचेगी। 💡 Insider Tip: Wet clothes (rain में भीगे) — temple entry OK but dripping wet नहीं। Monsoon season (June-Sept) में extra dupatta/towel carry करें। Rain stop होने पर थोड़ा dry होकर queue join करें। Temple inside slippery हो सकता है — careful walk।

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Temple inside selfie लेने की कोशिश: TTD staff immediately phone लेकर side रख देते हैं — return after darshan। Embarrassing situation + darshan experience disrupted। Just enjoy with eyes — memories permanent।
  • ❌ "VIP treatment" expect करना: Tirumala में भगवान सबके equal। ₹300 ticket = faster queue, but sanctum inside same 10-15 seconds everyone gets। Humility best attitude — divine experience rich बनती है।
  • ❌ Queue में phone charge करना (power bank): Technically allowed but impractical — crowd dense, bag opening difficult। Phone low battery concern? Airplane mode रखें queue से पहले — battery 6+ hours easily last करेगी।
  • ❌ Children को unsupervised छोड़ना: Crowd massive — children easily lost। Always hand-hold। TTD lost & found counter है but — prevention better। Wrist band with phone number लगाएँ।
  • ❌ Arguments with TTD staff: Staff strict but doing their job — thousands manage करते हैं daily। Cooperate, smile, follow instructions। Arguments = time waste + stress। Respectful behaviour = smoother experience।
