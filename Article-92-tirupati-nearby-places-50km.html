क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Padmavathi Temple skip करना: "Time nahi hai" — just 5 km, 1 hour total. Spiritual tradition: Padmavathi darshan first, then Tirumala darshan = complete pilgrimage. Auto ₹100 — don't skip।
  • ❌ Chandragiri Fort Friday visit plan: Fort museum closed on Fridays! Confirm opening day before going. Saturday-Thursday = open 10 AM-5 PM. Sound & Light show evening (check timings — seasonal)।
  • ❌ Srikalahasti puja without advance booking: Walk-in = long wait (2-3 hours) for Rahu-Ketu puja. Online/phone booking available — saves 2 hours. Just darshan (no puja) = quick, no booking needed।
  • ❌ Talakona visit in summer expecting waterfall: April-June = minimal water flow, hot forest. Best: August-October (post-monsoon peak). Confirm locally before planning — if no rain week, waterfall dry।
  • ❌ Too many places one day attempt: Tirumala darshan itself = exhausting. Next day 2-3 nearby places max. Don't try 5 places — rushing ruins experience. Pick 2-3 based on interest — enjoy fully।
