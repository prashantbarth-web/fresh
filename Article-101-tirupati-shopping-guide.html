⚠️ जरूरी बात: Sandalwood items — fake common! Cheap "sandalwood" = often chemically scented regular wood। Real sandalwood expensive (₹800+ for small piece)। If price too good to be true = probably fake। Buy from LEPAKSHI (govt shop) या established stores with bill। Sniff test: real sandalwood fragrance subtle, long-lasting; fake = strong chemical smell that fades quickly। 💡 Insider Tip: LEPAKSHI Emporium (AP government handicraft shop) = best for authentic sandalwood, Kondapalli toys, Kalamkari paintings, bidri work। Fixed government prices — no bargaining needed, no fake risk, bill guaranteed। Quality assured। Located in city — ask auto "LEPAKSHI shop"। Great for meaningful souvenirs।

Bargaining Tips — How to Get Fair Price

  • ✅ Walk away technique: Quote 50% of asking price → shop owner counters → settle 30-40% off. If they let you walk = price genuine, come back।
  • ✅ Compare 2-3 shops: Car Street has 20+ similar shops — compare prices before buying. Competition = better deals।
  • ✅ Buy bulk: Multiple items same shop = better discount. "5 items le raha hun — package price do" works well।
  • ✅ Ask for bill: Bill = accountability. No bill shops = more likely to overcharge. Bill asking itself reduces quoted price (tax compliance pressure)।
  • ❌ Don't show excitement: "Wah bahut sundar!" immediately = price increases. Stay neutral, compare, then decide।

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Tirumala hill पर shopping करना: Captive audience = inflated prices (30-50% more)। Same items city में cheaper + more variety। Buy in Tirupati city only — before going up or after returning।
  • ❌ Fake sandalwood खरीदना: ₹100-₹200 "sandalwood" item = definitely fake (real sandalwood ₹800+ minimum for small piece)। Chemical scented regular wood disguised। LEPAKSHI govt shop = authenticated. Burn test: real sandalwood — white ash, continues fragrance; fake — black ash, chemical smell।
  • ❌ First shop से without comparison खरीदना: 20+ shops same street — prices vary 20-40%। Walk entire stretch (10 min), note prices mentally, then buy from best value shop। First shop = usually not cheapest।
  • ❌ Heavy brass items buy करना without transport plan: 2-3 kg brass lamp = carrying problem in train/bus. Plan luggage capacity before buying heavy items. Shipping available some shops — ask। Or buy lightweight alternatives (small items, photos, books)।
  • ❌ TTD official laddus third-party से खरीदना: Streets पर "extra laddu ₹100" sellers = questionable authenticity. Only TTD counters (post-darshan area) sell genuine laddus. Price ₹50 official. Street sellers = possibly old/fake/unhygienic. Don't risk sacred prasadam quality।
