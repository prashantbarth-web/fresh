Hundi में कितना डालें? Tax benefit मिलेगा? Annadanam sponsor कर सकते हैं? Gold/Silver कैसे donate करें?" सब answers यहाँ मिलेंगे। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — TTD Donation:
  • Online Donation: TTD website → e-Hundi section → amount enter → payment → receipt (tax deduction eligible)
  • Offline (Tirumala): Hundi boxes temple area में — cash/gold/silver directly deposit
  • Annadanam Sponsorship: ₹500 onwards — free meals sponsor for pilgrims
  • Tax Benefit: 80G exemption available — official receipt mandatory
  • Minimum amount: कोई minimum नहीं — ₹1 से लेकर करोड़ों तक
  • Schemes: e-Hundi, SV Annadanam Trust, Go Samrakshana, Education, Medical

💡 Insider Tip: अगर tax benefit चाहिए, तो Hundi box में cash मत डालें — e-Hundi (online) use करें। Hundi box anonymous है (no receipt = no tax claim)। e-Hundi से PAN-linked receipt मिलता है — income tax return में 80G deduction claim कर सकते हैं।

Online Donation (e-Hundi) — Step-by-Step

Step 1: TTD Website पर जाएँ

  • Website: tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in
  • "e-Hundi" या "Donation" section ढूंढें

Step 2: Login / Register

  • Existing TTD account से login करें
  • New user: Mobile + email + basic details से register

Step 3: Donation Type Select करें

Option Purpose Impact
e-Hundi General temple fund Temple maintenance, sevas, activities
Annadanam Trust Free food for pilgrims Directly feeds thousands daily
Go Samrakshana Cow shelter maintenance TTD goshala — feeding + care
Education Fund TTD-run schools/colleges Students' education support
SV Medical Trust TTD hospitals/medical camps Free medical care for pilgrims

Step 4: Amount Enter करें

  • Minimum: ₹1 (technically) — practical: ₹100+
  • कोई maximum limit नहीं
  • Round figures common (₹500, ₹1,000, ₹5,000, ₹11,000)
  • अपनी श्रद्धा और capacity अनुसार दें

Step 5: PAN Details (Tax Benefit के लिए)

  • PAN number enter करें — 80G receipt generate होगा
  • PAN not mandatory — but tax claim के लिए ज़रूरी
  • Name exactly as per PAN card fill करें

Step 6: Payment करें

  • UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking — सब accepted
  • Payment successful → Instant confirmation

Step 7: Receipt Download

  • Payment success → Digital receipt generate
  • Email पर भी receipt copy आएगी
  • 80G certificate separately available — download section
  • ITR filing time use करें
⚠️ जरूरी बात: e-Hundi donation सिर्फ TTD official website से करें। Third-party collection agents, random UPI IDs, WhatsApp links — सब fraud हो सकते हैं। TTD ने कोई third-party authorized नहीं किया। Official website = safe।

Tax Benefits — 80G Deduction

Aspect Details
Section Income Tax Act Section 80G
Deduction 50% of donation amount (check current rules)
Eligibility Any Indian taxpayer (individual, HUF, company)
Requirement Official TTD receipt with PAN + donation certificate
Applicable On e-Hundi, Annadanam, Education, Medical trusts
NOT Applicable Physical Hundi cash (no receipt = no claim)
How to Claim ITR filing → Section 80G → TTD receipt upload
Limit Subject to income tax rules (percentage of gross income)
💡 Insider Tip: Financial year end (January-March) में donation करना smart है — tax planning complete होती है। ₹10,000 donation पर approximately ₹1,500-₹3,000 tax save हो सकता है (depending on tax slab)। Spiritual satisfaction + financial benefit — win-win।

Offline Donation — Tirumala/Tirupati पर

1. Hundi Box (Temple Inside)

  • Temple sanctum area में Hundi (donation box) placed हैं
  • Cash, coins, gold ornaments — directly deposit
  • Anonymous — no receipt, no name recorded
  • Most traditional form of donation
  • Tax benefit: ❌ (no proof/receipt)

2. Donation Counter (Receipt-based)

  • Tirumala पर separate donation counters available
  • Cash/DD/Cheque accept करते हैं
  • Official receipt with name, amount, PAN (if provided)
  • Tax benefit: ✅ (80G eligible — receipt = proof)
  • Location: Near temple admin area — staff guide करेगी

3. Gold/Silver Donation

Item Process Receipt
Gold ornaments Donation counter → Weigh → Valuation → Receipt Weight + purity + valuation certificate
Silver items Same process as gold Weight + valuation receipt
Gold coins/bars Counter → Verification → Receipt Weight + purity certificate
Precious stones Evaluation by TTD jeweler Valuation certificate

Annadanam Donation — Free Meals Sponsor

सबसे impactful donation — आपके पैसे से भूखे pilgrims को free food मिलता है:
Amount Impact How
₹500 ~25 pilgrims को 1 meal Online / Counter
₹1,000 ~50 pilgrims को 1 meal Online / Counter
₹5,000 ~250 pilgrims को 1 meal Online / Counter
₹10,000 ~500 pilgrims को 1 meal Online / Counter
₹1,00,000 Full day Annadanam sponsorship (thousands of meals) Special booking — TTD office
💡 Insider Tip: Annadanam donation most visible impact वाला donation है — TTD daily 3-4 lakh लोगों को free food serve करती है। आपका ₹500 = 25 लोगों का पेट भरा। Plus: Annadanam hall में donor's name (optional) display board पर दिखता है specific date को — spiritual satisfaction immense।

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Hundi box में large cash डालकर tax claim करना: Hundi anonymous है — कोई receipt नहीं मिलती। Tax benefit चाहिए तो e-Hundi (online) या donation counter (receipt-based) use करें। बड़ी amount donate कर रहे हैं तो receipt ज़रूर लें।
  • ❌ Third-party websites/agents से donation करना: सिर्फ TTD official website (tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in) safe है। Random UPI IDs, WhatsApp collection groups, Facebook pages — fraud common है। TTD ने कोई third-party authorize नहीं किया।
  • ❌ PAN details न देना (tax benefit चाहिए तो): 80G receipt PAN-linked होता है। बिना PAN donation कर सकते हैं, लेकिन ITR में deduction claim नहीं कर पाएँगे। PAN ज़रूर enter करें अगर tax saving plan है।
  • ❌ Receipt save न करना: Online donation receipt email + website दोनों पर available है — download करके safe रखें। ITR filing (July next year) तक ज़रूरत पड़ सकती है। Print copy + digital copy दोनों रखें।
  • ❌ Peer pressure में ज़्यादा donate करना: Donation श्रद्धा और capacity अनुसार करें — कोई minimum/maximum compulsion नहीं है। ₹10 भी उतना ही valuable है भगवान के लिए जितना ₹10 lakh। Financial strain लेकर donate करने की ज़रूरत नहीं।
