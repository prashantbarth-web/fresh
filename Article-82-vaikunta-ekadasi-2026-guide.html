क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Day 1 ही attend करने की ज़िद: "Ekadasi Day 1 = maximum punya" — TTD clarifies ALL 10 days equal merit। Day 1 crowd = unmanageable, physical risk (stampede incidents past). Day 5-10 = same darshan, fraction crowd। Rational choice।
  • ❌ Without booking same-day travel: Ekadasi morning Tirupati पहुँचे — hill bus queue 4 hours, darshan queue 12 hours = impossible to complete in one day। Arrive day before (minimum) — settle, rest, join queue 2-3 AM fresh।
  • ❌ Warm clothes न carry करना: January Tirumala = 10-15°C nights, wind chill factor। Queue 2 AM onwards = freezing for 6-8 hours open-air। Jacket, shawl, warm socks — essential। Hypothermia cases reported among unprepared devotees।
  • ❌ Fasting strictly during queue: Ekadasi = fasting tradition। But 10-15 hours physical exertion on empty stomach = dangerous (dizziness, fainting). Compromise: light fruits/milk (allowed in Ekadasi fast tradition) carry। Health first — God understands।
  • ❌ Whole family (elderly + kids) bringing on Day 1: Extreme crowd not suitable for senior citizens or young children. Safety risk real. Options: elderly paid entry (₹300), kids avoid Day 1-2, or whole family go Day 5-10 together safely।
