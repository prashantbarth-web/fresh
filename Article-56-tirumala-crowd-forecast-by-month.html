Month-wise darshan time, best time to visit और crowd calendar हिंदी में। WP TAGS : tirumala-crowd-calendar, tirupati-best-time-visit, tirumala-less-crowd-month, ttd-darshan-waiting-time, tirumala-crowd-forecast CATEGORY : pilgrimage-tips "Tirupati कब जाएँ जब भीड़ कम हो?" — यह सबसे practical सवाल है जो हर pilgrim पूछता है। क्योंकि भीड़ = लंबी queue = ज़्यादा wait = exhausting experience। अगर सही month और सही day choose करें, तो 8 hours की queue 2-3 hours में हो सकती है! मैंने Tirumala 4 बार visit किया — February (कम crowd, 2.5 hours darshan), October (moderate, 4 hours), December (Vaikunta Ekadasi, 14 hours!), और June (monsoon, 3 hours)। Difference dramatic है। इस article में month-by-month crowd data, best days, और smart planning tips share कर रहा हूँ। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — Tirumala Crowd Calendar:
  • Least Crowd: February, March, June, July, August — weekdays
  • Maximum Crowd: October-January (festival season), especially Vaikunta Ekadasi
  • Best Days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday — weekdays always less than weekends
  • Worst Days: Saturday, Sunday, Public Holidays, Festival days
  • Darshan Time (Low Crowd): 2-3 hours (Sarvadarshan) / 1-1.5 hours (₹300 ticket)
  • Darshan Time (Peak Crowd): 8-12+ hours (Sarvadarshan) / 3-6 hours (₹300 ticket)

Smart Planning Strategy — कम Crowd में Darshan

Best Combination (Minimum Wait):

  • 🎯 Month: February या March
  • 🎯 Day: Tuesday, Wednesday, या Thursday
  • 🎯 Time: Early morning queue (4-5 AM join)
  • 🎯 Ticket: ₹300 Special Entry (if available)
  • 🎯 Result: 1-2 hours darshan possible!

Worst Combination (Maximum Wait):

  • ❌ Month: December (Vaikunta Ekadasi period)
  • ❌ Day: Saturday/Sunday/Holiday
  • ❌ Time: Late morning join (10 AM+)
  • ❌ Ticket: Sarvadarshan (free — longest queue)
  • ❌ Result: 12-24+ hours wait possible!

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Weekend + Festival season plan करना: Saturday-Sunday already crowded + October-December festival = nightmare। Weekday + off-season = smart planning। 2-3 hours vs 8-12 hours — choice obvious है।
  • ❌ Late morning (9-11 AM) queue join करना: Maximum pilgrims 7-11 AM join करते हैं। Either early (2-5 AM) या late afternoon (3-5 PM) join करें — significantly less wait।
  • ❌ School holidays में plan करना (April-May): Families vacation पर Tirupati आती हैं — crowd doubles। Plus extreme heat (40°C+)। February-March much better option।
  • ❌ TTD crowd data check न करना: TTD website real-time darshan waiting time बताती है। Trip से 1-2 days पहले check करें — current pattern से estimate मिल जाएगा।
  • ❌ "कोई भी दिन चलेगा" approach: Planning makes massive difference। Same month में Tuesday vs Saturday = 3 hours vs 7 hours difference। Dates flexible रखें और optimize करें।
