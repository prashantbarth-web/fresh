First-timer confusion real है। Tirupati Railway Station busy है — but well-organized। Right exit, right transport = smooth start। मैंने 5 बार Tirupati station use किया — हर बार कुछ नया सीखा। Complete station navigation guide share कर रहा हूँ — platform से Tirumala bus stand तक। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — Station Arrival:
  • Station name: Tirupati (TPTY) — main station for pilgrims
  • Exit: Main exit → straight ahead for bus/auto/taxi
  • Tirumala bus: APSRTC bus stand 500m from station (walk 5 min)
  • Prepaid taxi: Available outside main exit — counter based, fixed rates
  • Luggage storage: Cloak room on Platform 1 — ₹20-₹30 per bag per day
  • Tip: Ignore touts inside station — go directly to prepaid counter

⚠️ जरूरी बात: Cloak room requires valid train ticket — अगर ticket digital है (phone) तो counter पर show करें। Bag must have lock — without lock acceptance denied (security)। Station पर ₹30-₹50 में lock available shops से। Valuables (laptop, expensive camera) avoid — basic luggage best for cloak room। 💡 Insider Tip: Best strategy: Station arrive → heavy bag cloak room (₹20-₹30) → sling bag + essentials only → bus to Tirumala → darshan → return same day → collect bag → hotel or departure। This way: no heavy bag carrying uphill, no locker dependency on hill, safe at station। Works perfectly for day-trip darshan plan।

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Station auto-wale से negotiate without prepaid check: Auto-wale demand ₹600-₹1000 for Tirumala (actual bus ₹75)। Always check prepaid counter first — government fixed rates। Or skip auto entirely — walk 5 min to bus stand, ₹75 bus every 10 min।
  • ❌ Renigunta station confuse करना Tirupati से: Some trains stop Renigunta Junction (10 km before Tirupati)। Different station! Check your ticket — ensure destination "TPTY" (Tirupati) not "RU" (Renigunta)। If Renigunta: local train/auto to Tirupati (15 min, ₹10-₹150)।
  • ❌ Heavy luggage Tirumala ले जाना: Hill पर heavy bag = burden। Queue में large bags = locker compulsory (sometimes full). Better: station cloak room (₹20-₹30) → light bag uphill → return collect. Saves energy + stress।
  • ❌ Night arrival without plan: Late night train (11 PM - 3 AM) — buses available but finding bearings difficult for first-timers। Options: station waiting room (free, safe) → rest till 4-5 AM → bus to Tirumala (early = short queue). Or book hotel near station (₹500-₹800 — 500m radius)।
  • ❌ Touts following inside station: "Sir, AC room chahiye? Tirumala taxi? Hotel?" — ignore firmly. Walk to official prepaid counter or bus stand directly। Touts earn commission — overpriced services। Official counters = fixed rates, no bargaining needed।
