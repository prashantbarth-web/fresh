Content:Tirumala में private hotels नहीं हैं — यह fact ज्यादातर first-timers को shock करता है

बहुत लोग सोचते हैं कि Tirumala पहुँचकर "कोई अच्छा hotel ढूँढ लेंगे।" लेकिन reality यह है — Tirumala hilltop पर एक भी private hotel नहीं है। पूरा accommodation system TTD (Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams) control करता है। Cottages, guest houses, choultries — सब TTD managed हैं।

यह system actually भक्तों के favour में है — rates controlled हैं (₹100 से ₹2,000 तक), no middlemen, no overcharging। लेकिन catch यह है कि availability extremely limited है — demand हमेशा supply से 5-10 गुना ज्यादा। बिना advance planning के Tirumala में room मिलना almost lottery जीतने जैसा है।
<blockquote>⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — Tirumala Stay Options:
<ul>
 	<li>TTD Cottages: ₹500-2,000/night — best quality, advance online booking (60 days)</li>
 	<li>TTD Guest Houses: ₹200-600/night — decent rooms, limited availability</li>
 	<li>TTD Choultries: Free-₹100 — basic halls, first-come-first-serve</li>
 	<li>Pilgrim Rest Houses: Free — large dormitory style, no booking needed</li>
 	<li>Private hotels: NOT available on Tirumala hilltop</li>
 	<li>Alternative: <a href="/tirupati-budget-hotels-1000/">Tirupati hotels</a> (22 km below) — always available</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>

<h2>TTD Cottages — Tirumala का Best Accommodation</h2>
TTD Cottages Tirumala पर सबसे comfortable stay option हैं — AC rooms, attached bathroom, bed + bedding, और temple walking distance।
<h3>Types of TTD Cottages:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Single Room Cottage:</strong> ₹500-800/night — 1-2 persons, basic AC room</li>
 	<li><strong>Double Room Cottage:</strong> ₹800-1,200/night — family of 3-4, larger space</li>
 	<li><strong>Suite/VIP Cottage:</strong> ₹1,500-2,000/night — spacious, premium location, limited</li>
 	<li><strong>Location:</strong> Multiple complexes — Srivari Mettu area, near temple, near bus stand</li>
</ul>
<h3>कैसे Book करें — TTD Cottages:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Online:</strong> <a href="/ttd-accommodation-online-booking/">TTD official website</a> (tirupati.org → Accommodation) — opens 60 days in advance</li>
 	<li><strong>Timing:</strong> Slots open 10 AM IST — book within first 5-10 minutes (sells out fast)</li>
 	<li><strong>Required:</strong> TTD login account + darshan ticket linked (some quota linked to SED booking)</li>
 	<li><strong>Payment:</strong> Online only — UPI/card/net banking</li>
</ul>
⚠️ **जरूरी बात:** TTD cottages 60 days advance open होते हैं और literally 5-10 minutes में full हो जाते हैं। अगर serious हो तो 10 AM sharp पर login करके try करो — even 10:05 AM पर "no availability" दिख सकता है। Multiple devices से try करो family members के accounts से।
<h3>Cottage Availability Tips:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Weekdays (Mon-Thu) availability better — weekends almost impossible</li>
 	<li>Off-season (March-May summer) slightly easier — Oct-Jan full every day</li>
 	<li><a href="/ttd-special-entry-darshan-online-booking/">SED ₹300 ticket</a> holders को linked accommodation quota मिलता है — SED book करते time check करो</li>
 	<li>Cancellation check: Booking open date के 3-4 दिन बाद cancellations reflect होती हैं — daily recheck करो</li>
</ul>
<h2>TTD Guest Houses — Mid-Range Option</h2>
Guest houses cottages से slightly lower quality हैं — लेकिन clean और functional rooms मिलती हैं।
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Price:</strong> ₹200-600/night</li>
 	<li><strong>Room type:</strong> Non-AC/AC, attached bathroom, basic furniture</li>
 	<li><strong>Bedding:</strong> Usually provided — but thin mattress, own bedsheet recommended</li>
 	<li><strong>Location:</strong> Various complexes around Tirumala town</li>
</ul>
<h3>Booking:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Online:</strong> Same TTD website — 60 days advance, similar rush as cottages</li>
 	<li><strong>Offline counter:</strong> CRO (Central Reception Office) Tirumala — walk-in availability check</li>
 	<li><strong>CRO location:</strong> Near Tirumala bus stand — reach early morning (5-6 AM) for best chance</li>
 	<li><strong>Wait time at CRO:</strong> 30 min to 3+ hours depending on day/season</li>
</ul>
💡 **Insider Tip:** CRO counter पर morning 5-6 AM पहुँचो — previous night check-outs reflect होते हैं, fresh rooms available आते हैं। 9-10 AM तक जाओगे तो "no rooms" मिलेगा। Early bird advantage real है।

<h2>TTD Choultries — Free/Minimal Cost Option</h2>
Choultries large halls हैं जहाँ pilgrims floor पर या basic cots पर rest करते हैं। Private room नहीं — shared space।
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Price:</strong> Free या ₹50-100 (nominal)</li>
 	<li><strong>What you get:</strong> Covered space, shared toilet/bathroom, basic lighting, fan</li>
 	<li><strong>Privacy:</strong> None — large hall with 50-200 people</li>
 	<li><strong>Bedding:</strong> Not provided — अपना bedsheet/blanket ज़रूर carry करो</li>
 	<li><strong>Luggage:</strong> No locker — bags अपने पास रखो</li>
</ul>
<h3>Choultry Tips:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Booking:</strong> No advance booking — walk-in only, first-come basis</li>
 	<li><strong>Best time to get space:</strong> Morning 6-8 AM (previous pilgrims vacate)</li>
 	<li><strong>Duration:</strong> Usually 12-24 hours max — not long-term</li>
 	<li><strong>Clean ones:</strong> Newer choultries (built after 2015) better maintained — ask staff</li>
</ul>
⚠️ **जरूरी बात:** Choultries comfortable नहीं हैं — लेकिन safe हैं (TTD managed, CCTV, guards)। अगर darshan queue 12-15 hours है और room नहीं मिला — choultry में 4-5 hours rest करके queue join करना practical approach है। Expectations low रखो — यह "hotel" नहीं है, "shelter" है।
<h2>Pilgrim Rest Houses — Completely Free</h2>
TTD कुछ large dormitory-style rest houses maintain करता है जहाँ कोई भी pilgrim free में rest कर सकता है।
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Price:</strong> Completely free</li>
 	<li><strong>Space:</strong> Large halls — floor sleeping, 100+ capacity</li>
 	<li><strong>Facilities:</strong> Shared toilets, drinking water, fan</li>
 	<li><strong>Duration:</strong> Few hours rest — not overnight stay (technically)</li>
 	<li><strong>Availability:</strong> Always open — space milta hai but comfort zero</li>
</ul>
💡 **Insider Tip:** अगर Divya Darshan token लिया है (<a href="/alipiri-footpath-trek-guide/">Alipiri trek</a> करके आए) और 2-3 hours wait है token time तक — rest house perfect है quick nap के लिए। Long stay के लिए suitable नहीं।
<h2>Room नहीं मिला Tirumala पर — Plan B क्या है?</h2>
Reality accept करो — ज्यादातर pilgrims को Tirumala पर room नहीं मिलता। यह normal है। Plan B ready रखो:
<h3>Option 1: Queue में ही रात काटो</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><a href="/vqc-vaikuntam-queue-complex-guide/">VQC (Vaikuntam Queue Complex)</a> में floor पर space मिलता है — fans, toilets, drinking water available</li>
 	<li>Queue में bedsheet/thin mattress spread करो — 4-6 hours sleep possible</li>
 	<li>Advantage: Queue advance होती रहती है — time waste नहीं</li>
</ul>
<h3>Option 2: Tirumala open areas</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Temple town में covered areas (bus stand, mandapams) में rest possible</li>
 	<li>Night temperature pleasant (15-22°C) — blanket/shawl carry करो</li>
 	<li>Safe — TTD security patrols active 24/7</li>
</ul>
<h3>Option 3: Tirupati लौटकर hotel</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Tirumala → Tirupati bus (last bus ~10 PM, first bus ~3:30 AM) — 45 min</li>
 	<li><a href="/tirupati-railway-station-hotels/">Tirupati station area hotels</a> always available — ₹600-1,500</li>
 	<li>Next morning fresh होकर Tirumala वापस आओ darshan के लिए</li>
</ul>
<h3>Option 4: Tirupati में pre-book करके जाओ</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Smart approach — <a href="/tirupati-budget-hotels-1000/">Tirupati hotel</a> advance book करो (guaranteed room)</li>
 	<li>Darshan day early morning Tirumala जाओ, darshan करो, same day Tirupati return</li>
 	<li>No stress about Tirumala accommodation — plan fixed</li>
</ul>
💡 **Insider Tip:** मेरी personal recommendation — Tirumala पर room मिलने पर depend मत करो। Tirupati में hotel book करो (confirmed), darshan day Tirumala जाओ bus से (5 AM), darshan complete करो, शाम तक Tirupati return। Stress-free approach — room guarantee + darshan done।
<h2>Tirumala Accommodation — Complete Booking Strategy</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>60 days before:</strong> TTD website पर cottage/guest house online booking try करो — 10 AM sharp</li>
 	<li><strong>45-30 days before:</strong> Daily cancellation check करो TTD website पर — cancelled rooms reappear करते हैं</li>
 	<li><strong>7 days before:</strong> अगर online नहीं मिला — Tirupati hotel backup book करो (MakeMyTrip/OYO)</li>
 	<li><strong>Tirumala पहुँचकर:</strong> CRO counter जाओ morning 5-6 AM — offline availability check</li>
 	<li><strong>CRO भी "no room":</strong> Choultry/rest house use करो या Tirupati hotel plan activate करो</li>
</ul>
<h2>Tirumala Stay — क्या Expect करें (Honest Picture)</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>TTD Cottage (best case):</strong> Clean AC room, bed + pillow, attached bathroom, hot water, TV — comfortable 1-2 night stay। Location temple walking distance।</li>
 	<li><strong>Guest House:</strong> Functional room, basic bed, attached/shared bathroom। Clean but not luxurious — purpose serve होता है।</li>
 	<li><strong>Choultry:</strong> Large hall, floor/mat sleeping, shared toilets, fans। Privacy zero — "camping in a hall" feel।</li>
 	<li><strong>Rest House:</strong> Sit/lie down space in a large hall। Rest for few hours only — not sleeping overnight।</li>
</ul>
⚠️ **जरूरी बात:** Tirumala accommodation को "hotel" मत समझो — यह pilgrim service है। Rooms functional हैं, luxurious नहीं। Expectations set करो accordingly — darshan ही main purpose है, room secondary। Clean bed, safe environment — यही काफी है।
<h2>Tirumala पर Stay करने के Rules</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Check-in:</strong> ID proof mandatory (Aadhaar/Voter ID)</li>
 	<li><strong>Duration:</strong> Maximum 24 hours (extend possible if availability, but not guaranteed)</li>
 	<li><strong>Check-out:</strong> Strict timing — late checkout penalty/eviction possible</li>
 	<li><strong>Food inside room:</strong> Allowed (vegetarian only) — but keep room clean</li>
 	<li><strong>Alcohol/Non-veg:</strong> Strictly prohibited — eviction + penalty if caught</li>
 	<li><strong>Smoking:</strong> Prohibited entire Tirumala area</li>
 	<li><strong>Noise:</strong> Quiet after 10 PM — respect other pilgrims</li>
 	<li><strong><a href="/tirumala-dress-code-rules/">Dress code</a>:</strong> Traditional/modest — same rules as temple area</li>
</ul>
<h2>Tirumala में Amenities — Room के अलावा क्या Available है</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Food:</strong> <a href="/tirumala-annadanam-guide/">Annadanam (free meals)</a> — breakfast, lunch, dinner available free for all pilgrims</li>
 	<li><strong>Drinking water:</strong> RO water multiple points — free</li>
 	<li><strong>Toilets/Bathrooms:</strong> Public toilets maintained throughout — reasonably clean</li>
 	<li><strong>Locker:</strong> <a href="/tirumala-mobile-locker-guide/">Mobile/valuables locker</a> — ₹10-50 per use</li>
 	<li><strong>Medical:</strong> TTD hospital + first aid — free for pilgrims</li>
 	<li><strong>Shopping:</strong> Basic items (prasad boxes, religious items) — TTD shops</li>
</ul>
<h2>आम गलतियाँ — Common Mistakes</h2>
<ul>
 	<li>❌ <strong>"Tirumala पर hotel book कर लूँगा" assume करना</strong> — Tirumala पर private hotels exist नहीं करते। सारा accommodation TTD managed है — advance booking ज़रूरी या offline counter try। "Hotel ढूँढ लेंगे" mindset = रात open area में काटना।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Online booking 10 AM के 15 min बाद try करना</strong> — TTD cottages literally 5-10 min में full हो जाती हैं। 10:00 AM sharp login + fastest click = only chance। 10:15 AM = "no availability"।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Bedsheet/blanket carry नहीं करना</strong> — Guest houses में provided होती है (thin), choultries में नहीं। अपनी light blanket/bedsheet ज़रूर लेकर जाओ — Tirumala रात में cold हो जाता है (15-18°C winter में)।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>CRO counter दोपहर 12 बजे जाना</strong> — Morning 5-6 AM जाओ — fresh check-outs reflect होते हैं। Afternoon तक सब allot हो चुका होता है। Early morning = better chance।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Tirumala accommodation के भरोसे Tirupati hotel cancel करना</strong> — Tirumala room guaranteed नहीं है until confirmed booking in hand। Tirupati hotel backup हमेशा रखो — worst case Tirupati return हो सकता है।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>24 hour check-out rule ignore करना</strong> — TTD rooms strict time-bound हैं। Late vacate = next pilgrim को problem + आपको penalty। Check-out time note करो और follow करो।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Valuable items room में छोड़कर darshan जाना</strong> — TTD rooms safe हैं mostly, but कोई insurance नहीं है। Cash, jewellery, electronics body पर रखो या TTD locker use करो।</li>
</ul>
