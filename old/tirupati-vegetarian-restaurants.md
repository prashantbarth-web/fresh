<h2>Tirupati Food Scene — Basic Understanding</h2>
Tirupati temple town है — food culture पूरी तरह vegetarian dominated है।
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Vegetarian majority:</strong> 90%+ restaurants pure vegetarian — finding non-veg very difficult (and not recommended temple town में)</li>
 	<li><strong>South Indian dominant:</strong> Rice-based meals primary — idli, dosa, vada, sambar rice, rasam rice, curd rice</li>
 	<li><strong>North Indian available:</strong> Select restaurants — roti, dal, paneer — but not everywhere</li>
 	<li><strong>Pricing:</strong> Very affordable — temple town economics, pilgrim-friendly rates</li>
 	<li><strong>Timings:</strong> Early morning 5-6 AM onwards (pilgrim schedule) — late night limited (after 10 PM)</li>
 	<li><strong>Hygiene:</strong> Generally good — temple town standards, competition keeps quality up</li>
</ul>
💡 **Insider Tip:** Tirupati में "meals" order करो restaurant में — यह unlimited rice + sambar + rasam + curd + vegetables + papad + pickle plate है। ₹80-120 में full stomach guaranteed। North India का "thali" concept — but South Indian style। Value for money unbeatable।
<h2>Top Vegetarian Restaurants — Area-Wise</h2>
<h3>Railway Station Area (Convenience — Late Night Arrivals)</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Laxmi Narayana Bhavan:</strong> Pure South Indian, ₹50-150, 6 AM-10 PM, station 200m</li>

 	<li><strong>Sri Venkateshwara Bhavan:</strong> Budget meals, ₹60-100, quick service, always crowded (good sign)</li>

<h3>Karakambadi Road (Quality — Family Dining)</h3>

 	<li><strong>Maya Restaurant (Marasa Sarovar):</strong> Hotel restaurant, ₹200-400, variety, comfortable ambiance</li>
 	<li><strong>Sri Krishna Bhavan:</strong> Authentic South Indian, ₹80-150, popular with locals (quality indicator)</li>

</ul>
<h3>Bus Stand Area (Tirumala Bus Proximity)</h3>

<h3>Tirumala Hilltop (Limited Options — TTD Dominated)</h3>

<h2>North Indian Food कहाँ मिलता है? — Hindi Belt Pilgrims के लिए</h2>
North India से आने वालों का सबसे बड़ा concern: "Roti-sabzi मिलेगी कहीं?"
<h3>North Indian Food Available At:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Hotel Annapurna Restaurant:</strong> Roti, dal, paneer butter masala, aloo gobi — decent North Indian</li>
 	<li><strong>Minerva Grand:</strong> Extensive North Indian section — naan, dal makhani, shahi paneer</li>
 	<li><strong>Pai Viceroy:</strong> North Indian thali available — chapati + sabzi + dal + rice</li>
 	<li><strong>Maya Restaurant:</strong> Good North Indian options — paneer dishes, rajma, chole</li>
 	<li><strong>Select 3-star hotel restaurants:</strong> Most <a href="/tirupati-3-star-hotels/">3-star hotels</a> North Indian menu carry करते हैं</li>
</ul>
<h3>Reality Check — North Indian Food Quality:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Available है — but Delhi/Lucknow quality expect मत करो</li>
 	<li>Roti often slightly different texture — South Indian wheat different</li>
 	<li>Spice level adjusted for South palate — less mirch, more tamarind influence</li>
 	<li>"Paneer butter masala" मिलेगा — but taste slightly Andhra-fied</li>
 	<li>Best approach: Mix and match — 1 meal North Indian, 1 meal try South Indian</li>
</ul>
💡 **Insider Tip:** मेरी honest recommendation — Tirupati में South Indian food try करो। Ghee dosa, masala dosa, filter coffee, sambar vada — यह world-class food है और fresh बनता है। "Apna khana" attachment 2-3 days छोड़ो — new experience enjoy करो। लौटकर roti-sabzi तो मिल ही जाएगी!
<h2>Budget Food — ₹100 से कम में Full Meal</h2>
Tirupati pilgrims generally budget-conscious होते हैं — good news: यहाँ excellent food बहुत सस्ता मिलता है।
<h3>Budget Options:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>"Meals" (unlimited thali):</strong> ₹70-120 — rice + sambar + rasam + curd + 2 vegetables + papad + pickle। All unlimited refills।</li>
 	<li><strong>Morning tiffin:</strong> ₹30-60 — idli (4 pcs) + sambar/chutney, या dosa + chutney</li>
 	<li><strong>Vada + filter coffee:</strong> ₹40-50 — perfect snack combo</li>
 	<li><strong>Pongal + sambar:</strong> ₹50-70 — South Indian breakfast favourite</li>
 	<li><strong>Curd rice + pickle:</strong> ₹40-50 — simple, cooling, stomach-friendly (best for upset stomach)</li>
 	<li><strong>TTD Annadanam (Tirumala):</strong> ₹0 (FREE) — unlimited meals, all day</li>
</ul>
<h3>Budget Strategy:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Breakfast:</strong> Local bhavan (₹40-60) — idli/dosa/pongal + coffee</li>
 	<li><strong>Lunch:</strong> "Meals" at any restaurant (₹80-120) — full unlimited thali</li>
 	<li><strong>Dinner:</strong> Light — dosa or tiffin (₹50-70) or hotel room service (₹100-150)</li>
 	<li><strong>Total day:</strong> ₹170-350 per person food — extremely affordable</li>
</ul>

<h2>Eating Etiquette — Temple Town में</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Hand eating:</strong> South Indian meals traditionally right hand से — spoon/fork available पूछने पर, but try eating with hand (authentic experience)</li>
 	<li><strong>Unlimited refills:</strong> "Meals" में waiter आएगा refill देने — hand gesture या "haan" बोलो for more, "bas" for stop</li>
 	<li><strong>Banana leaf:</strong> कुछ traditional restaurants banana leaf पर serve करते हैं — eco-friendly, adds flavour</li>
 	<li><strong>Water:</strong> Packaged water prefer करो — or ask for "filtered water" (RO)</li>
 	<li><strong>Tipping:</strong> Not expected in budget restaurants — mid-range: ₹20-50 sufficient</li>
 	<li><strong>Timing respect:</strong> Lunch rush 12-2 PM extreme — 11:30 AM या 2:30 PM जाओ less crowd</li>
</ul>
<h2>Food Timing — Pilgrim Schedule Friendly</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Early morning (4-6 AM):</strong> Some bhavans open early — tiffin available for early Tirumala bus catchers</li>
 	<li><strong>Breakfast (6-10 AM):</strong> Peak tiffin time — idli, dosa, pongal, vada everywhere</li>
 	<li><strong>Lunch (11 AM-3 PM):</strong> "Meals" time — unlimited thali, most popular meal</li>
 	<li><strong>Snack (3-5 PM):</strong> Dosa, vada, coffee — light options</li>
 	<li><strong>Dinner (7-10 PM):</strong> Tiffin or meals — most restaurants close 10-10:30 PM</li>
 	<li><strong>Late night (post 10 PM):</strong> Very limited — hotel room service, station canteen only reliable</li>
</ul>
⚠️ **जरूरी बात:** Tirumala bus पकड़ने के लिए 4-5 AM निकलना है तो previous night dinner time ही next morning snack pack कर लो — इतनी early में open restaurants ढूँढना difficult। Bananas, dry snacks, biscuits keep करो room में emergency food।
<h2>Food Safety Tips — Pilgrims के लिए</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Water:</strong> Always packaged/filtered — tap water avoid</li>
 	<li><strong>Street food:</strong> Busy stalls choose (high turnover = fresh) — empty stalls avoid</li>
 	<li><strong>Spice level:</strong> Andhra cuisine SPICY है — "less spicy" specifically बोलो order करते time</li>
 	<li><strong>Stomach issue:</strong> Curd rice + banana — safest recovery food। Pharmacies everywhere — Electral ORS packets carry करो</li>
 	<li><strong>First day:</strong> Light food recommended — travel fatigue + new cuisine = digestion stress। Heavy meals avoid Day 1</li>
 	<li><strong>Darshan day:</strong> Light breakfast, avoid heavy/spicy — 6-12 hours queue में heavy food = acidity/gas</li>
</ul>
<h2>आम गलतियाँ — Common Mistakes</h2>
<ul>
 	<li>❌ <strong>"South Indian food मुझे पसंद नहीं" बोलकर sirf North Indian ढूँढते रहना</strong> — Limited North Indian options ढूँढने में time waste + average quality मिलेगा। Open mind रखो — fresh dosa, sambar vada, filter coffee = life-changing food experience। Try करो पहले।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Tirumala पर food arrangements plan नहीं करना</strong> — Tirumala hilltop पर private restaurants almost नहीं हैं। TTD Annadanam (free) और TTD canteens ही main options — accept this reality, Annadanam time plan करो।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Full spicy Andhra meal Day 1 पर खाना</strong> — Andhra food North Indian palate से significantly spicier है। First day "less spicy" specifically demand करो — otherwise stomach upset guaranteed। Gradually increase।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Darshan day morning heavy breakfast करना</strong> — Heavy meal + 6-12 hours standing queue = acidity, gas, restroom urgency (queue से निकलना = spot lose)। Light breakfast — idli, banana, biscuit sufficient।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Packaged water carry नहीं करना</strong> — Temple town water generally safe but body new environment = sensitivity possible। Packaged water first 2-3 days mandatory। ₹20 bottle = health insurance।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Late night food plan बिना hotel room service check किए</strong> — 10 PM बाद Tirupati shut हो जाता है mostly। Hotel room service confirm करो check-in पर। Alternative: Bananas, dry snacks room में रखो।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Restaurant तो ढूँढा लेकिन "meals" order करना भूल गए</strong> — "Meals" (unlimited thali) सबसे value-for-money option है South Indian restaurant में। Menu की individual items order करोगे तो 2x ज्यादा खर्च + less food। "Meals लगाओ" — one word solution।</li>
</ul>
