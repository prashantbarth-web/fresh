Reality? Tirupati में 3-star hotels ₹1,500-3,500 range में वो सब देते हैं जो एक pilgrim family को actually चाहिए — clean AC room, attached Western toilet, hot water, restaurant, parking, और safe environment। न budget hotels का compromise, न 5-star का unnecessary खर्चा।

मैंने personally 4 different 3-star hotels try किए हैं Tirupati में पिछले 3 सालों में — और honestly, ₹2,000 के 3-star room और ₹4,500 के "premium" room में practical difference सिर्फ swimming pool और fancy lobby का था। Room comfort almost same। यह article उन pilgrims के लिए है जो smart spend करना चाहते हैं — maximum comfort, minimum waste।

<h2>3-Star Hotel में क्या मिलता है — Detailed Breakdown</h2>
"3-star" term confusing हो सकता है — exactly क्या expect करना चाहिए Tirupati के 3-star hotels में:
<h3>Standard Facilities (almost सभी 3-star में):</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>✅ AC room — split AC, temperature control</li>
 	<li>✅ Attached bathroom — Western toilet + Indian both options usually</li>
 	<li>✅ 24/7 hot water — geyser in room</li>
 	<li>✅ Clean bed + quality mattress — fresh linens daily</li>
 	<li>✅ TV — cable/DTH channels</li>
 	<li>✅ WiFi — basic speed (enough for WhatsApp, maps)</li>
 	<li>✅ Elevator — multi-floor access</li>
 	<li>✅ In-house restaurant — veg food (South + North Indian)</li>
 	<li>✅ 24-hour front desk + room service</li>
 	<li>✅ Parking — car/bike space</li>
 	<li>✅ Luggage storage — darshan day bags safe</li>
 	<li>✅ Power backup — generator during outages</li>
</ul>
<h3>Bonus Facilities (select 3-star hotels):</h3>

<h2>Booking Tips — 3-Star Hotels Tirupati</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Best platforms:</strong> MakeMyTrip, Goibibo — filter "3-star", compare prices across platforms</li>
 	<li><strong>Advance booking:</strong> 15-30 days before — rates 20-30% cheaper than walk-in</li>
 	<li><strong>Free cancellation:</strong> Always select free cancellation option — ₹100-200 extra but flexibility invaluable</li>
 	<li><strong>Weekend vs Weekday:</strong> Friday-Sunday rates 30-50% higher — weekday travel significantly cheaper</li>
 	<li><strong>Peak season:</strong> Oct-Jan, Brahmotsavam, Vaikunta Ekadasi — rates double, book 45-60 days advance</li>
 	<li><strong>Coupon codes:</strong> First-time user discounts, bank offers — check before final payment</li>
 	<li><strong>Room type:</strong> "Deluxe" room ₹300-500 extra over "Standard" — usually bigger room + better view, worth it</li>
</ul>
<h3>Price Fluctuation Pattern:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Cheapest:</strong> Mon-Thu, March-May (summer off-season) — ₹1,200-2,000</li>
 	<li><strong>Moderate:</strong> Fri-Sun, June-September — ₹1,800-2,800</li>
 	<li><strong>Expensive:</strong> Oct-Jan weekends, festivals — ₹2,500-4,500</li>
 	<li><strong>Most expensive:</strong> Brahmotsavam, Vaikunta Ekadasi week — ₹3,500-6,000+</li>
</ul>

<h2>3-Star Hotel से Tirumala जाना — Logistics</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Tirumala bus:</strong> APSRTC bus stand से हर 5-10 min — hotel से auto ₹50-150 (distance dependent)</li>
 	<li><strong>Taxi to Tirumala:</strong> Hotel reception से arrange — ₹800-1,200 one-way (ghat road 22 km)</li>
 	<li><strong>Early morning plan:</strong> 4-5 AM wake-up → hotel breakfast (अगर available) → auto to bus stand → Tirumala bus</li>
 	<li><strong>Return:</strong> Tirumala → Tirupati bus (last ~10 PM) → auto to hotel</li>
 	<li><strong>Tip:</strong> Hotel reception से previous night ही auto/taxi morning के लिए arrange करवा लो — 4 AM पर street auto ढूँढना difficult</li>
</ul>

<h2>आम गलतियाँ — Common Mistakes</h2>
<ul>
 	<li>❌ <strong>सिर्फ price देखकर book करना — location ignore करना</strong> — ₹1,500 का 3-star hotel अगर Tirumala bus stand से 8 km दूर है तो daily auto ₹300 extra + inconvenience। Location check करो — bus stand से 3 km max।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Walk-in करना 3-star hotel में</strong> — Online rate ₹2,000, walk-in rate ₹2,800-3,000 same room। Always online book करो — 20-30% savings guaranteed।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>"Standard room" book करना family of 4 के लिए</strong> — Standard room usually 1 double bed = 2 adults comfortable, 4 tight। "Deluxe" या "Family" room select करो — ₹300-500 extra but space double।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Peak season में last week booking try करना</strong> — Brahmotsavam, Oct-Jan weekends — 3-star hotels 30-45 days advance full हो जाते हैं। Late booking = either "sold out" या inflated rates।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Review date check नहीं करना</strong> — 2022 में "excellent" hotel 2026 में "terrible" हो सकता है (ownership change, maintenance drop)। Last 3 months reviews ही trust करो।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Restaurant availability assume करना</strong> — कुछ "3-star" hotels restaurant temporarily closed रखते हैं off-season। Booking time confirm करो — "is restaurant operational?" Direct call करो hotel को।</li>
 	<li>❌ <strong>Check-in/out time mismatch ignore करना</strong> — Train 5 AM arrive, hotel check-in 12 PM = 7 hours waste। Early check-in ₹300-500 extra — booking time request करो, arrival day ही comfortable start हो।</li>
</ul>
