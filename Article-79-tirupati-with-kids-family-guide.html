"Queue में बच्चे manage होंगे? Food? Stroller? Infant के साथ कैसे?" Genuine concern — 3-6 hours queue + crowd + heat = kids difficult हो सकती है। लेकिन proper planning से family trip smooth होती है — TTD ने families/children के लिए कई facilities दी हैं। मैंने 2 बार बच्चों (4 साल और 7 साल) के साथ Tirumala visit किया — challenges real हैं, but manageable। Smart planning share कर रहा हूँ — age-wise tips, what to carry, और कैसे experience enjoyable बनाएँ। ⚡ संक्षिप्त उत्तर — Kids के साथ Tirupati:
  • Ticket: Children under 12 — free entry (no separate ticket needed)
  • Queue: Families with infants get priority — ask TTD staff at entry
  • Stroller: Not allowed in queue (narrow) — baby carrier recommended
  • Food: Baby food, milk, snacks — freely allowed to carry
  • Best timing: Early morning (3-5 AM) — shorter queue, cooler weather
  • Stay: Tirumala cottages best for families — book in advance

💡 Insider Tip: Baby carrier (front-facing) = lifesaver in Tirumala queue। Stroller absolutely not practical — narrow corridors, stairs, dense crowd। Baby carrier frees your hands, baby comfortable, और priority entry cases में staff immediately notice करते हैं (infant visible = faster process)।

क्या गलतियाँ करते हैं लोग (और कैसे avoid करें)

  • ❌ Stroller ले जाना Tirumala: Queue complex narrow paths, stairs, dense crowd — stroller useless + burden। Baby carrier (front/back) only practical option। Stroller hotel/car में छोड़ दें।
    • 👉 इसे भी पढ़ें: NRI darshan guide
  • ❌ Peak afternoon queue join करना with kids: 12-4 PM = hottest, most crowded। Children dehydrate, cry, fuss। Best: 3-5 AM (kids half-asleep, cool weather) or ₹300 paid entry (AC comfort)।
  • ❌ Extra snacks न carry करना: "Annadanam मिल जाएगा" — true, but queue inside food access zero। 3-6 hours wait — children need snacks every 1-2 hours। Biscuits, dry fruits, juice packs — carry double what you think needed।
  • ❌ Child ID/photo न रखना: Crowd में children lost ho sakte hain — scary but real। Wrist band with parent name + phone number + hotel name। Recent photo phone में — shows to security if separated। TTD lost-and-found counter active।
  • ❌ Festival days choose करना with toddlers: Brahmotsavam, Vaikunta Ekadasi = 50,000-1,00,000 people। Toddlers + extreme crowd = dangerous। Regular weekday trip safer and more enjoyable for families।
